Ford Mustang Wheelie

How to pop a wheelie consistently.

  1. Hold W + S together to engage Launch Control.
  2. Release S to launch, then immediately tap Shift (Clutch) to "rev" the car.
  3. Repeat to perfect the timing. Precision is required!
